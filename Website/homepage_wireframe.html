Stay informed with news and announcements from the anthroposophical world.

Anthroposophical Society of America Mar 15, 2026
New Waldorf School Opens in Austin, Texas

The Pinoak Waldorf School has officially opened its doors in South Austin, welcoming 78 students across grades K–8 in its inaugural year. The school represents a growing wave of new Waldorf initiatives across the southern United States.

Read More →
Demeter International Feb 28, 2026
Demeter Reports Record Biodynamic Farm Certifications in 2025

Demeter International announced that 2025 saw the highest number of new biodynamic farm certifications in its 98-year history, with over 1,200 new certified operations globally. Growth was strongest in North America, Brazil, and South Africa.

Read More →
The Goetheanum Feb 10, 2026
Goetheanum Announces Full 2026 Conference Program

The General Anthroposophical Society has released its comprehensive program for 2026, featuring over 40 events including the World Teachers Conference in April and the International Eurythmy Festival in August. Registration is now open for most events.
