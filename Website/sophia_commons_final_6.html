Goetheanum Launches New Global Online Study Program for 2026

The Goetheanum's Studium division has announced a major expansion of its online program, making anthroposophical study accessible to students worldwide. The spring 2026 cycle will include live weekly sessions, recorded lectures from leading practitioners, small discussion groups in six languages, and an artistic practice component.
